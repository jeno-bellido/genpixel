ABOUT US

GenPixel is the culmination of efforts by a team consisting of Jeno Bellido, Rey Anne Destua, Ariane Marie Lavilla, and John Cairo Minerva. These individuals are fourth-year students pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence at West Visayas State University. This website stands as the concluding project for their Computer Vision course, showcasing their proficiency and application of skills in the field.
